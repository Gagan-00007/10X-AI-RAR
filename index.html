About College & Departments

Amruta Institute of Engineering and Management Sciences

Amruta Institute of Engineering and Management Sciences (AIEMS) was established in 2008 in Bengaluru and is under the umbrella of Basaveshwara Vidya Vardhak Sangh (BVVS), Bagalkot. The Campus is on a sprawling 14.35 acres of land surrounded by lush green plantations in Bidadi, Bengaluru.

The Institution is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, India. AIEMS, Bidadi, Bengaluru has an excellent infrastructure with the state-of-the-art equipment and machinery as laboratory facilities.

At present AIEMS, Bengaluru has Six Under Graduate Engineering Programs along with Three Post Graduate Programs in Engineering and Business Administration. The Institute has highly qualified faculty, R&D programs, a centralized library with open access facility, and a digital library with online access to International Journals.

Organizing Departments

The Departments of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), and Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at AIEMS offer B.E. and M.Tech. programs designed to meet the growing demands of the AI-driven world.

The departments actively engage in enhancing student competencies through Workshops, Hackathons, Certification programs, Skill Labs, Idea Labs, and industry tie-ups through MoUs and Centers of Excellence (CoE).

Strengthened by well-qualified faculty members, well-equipped laboratories, departmental libraries, and the active IEEE-AIEMS Student Chapter, the departments aim to prepare students for cutting-edge technology with competence, commitment, and teamwork.
